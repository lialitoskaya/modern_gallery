Immerse yourself in mid-century modernism through unique interior objects.

Designed by designer He Mu and Zhang Qian from Shanghai University of Engineering Science, Sunflower Chair has won "Redtory Design Award" for the 5th "Design for Sitting" Grand Prix. Experts in the jury all agreed that the chair satisfied the very thing intention of sitting—reading, which meets the theme "sitting and other behaviors" quite well.

Stunning, breathtaking and very rare floor lamp by Tibor Hazi 1973. Talking about quality it doesn’t get much better. A real contemporary space age design. Tibor Hazi a skilled glass blower from Hungaria made this amazing floor lamp. The glass was blown at the Tokod Glass Factory and due to the red color it had to be send to Parad Glass Factory to be colored. Hazi cut the glass himself and assembled the lights in his workshop. The bases were bent by hand. The production of the lights was quite time consuming and the result of it is stunning.

One of the most iconic design pieces from the Space Age era is this Vision 2000 HiFi system from Rosita, designed by the famous Thilo Oerke 1971.
